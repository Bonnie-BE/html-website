Started in 2021, our vision was to provide quality, accessible learining to all, especially high scholars. We help with tutoring learners who are in high school, we have one of the best tutors that we train ourselves, and most of them have experience with tutoring. We also have classes on the weekends where we teach students about how the world works and technology, we teach them computer programming and computer literacy. We host a competition annually called "Game Changer", where we give our learners all around the country the opportunity to bring forth their ideas and showcase them in the competition.
